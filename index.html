Our Story

A Legacy Forged
in Gold

JKJ Jewellers began in 1868 in the small town of Ratangarh — a humble yet visionary start that would grow into one of India's most respected names in fine jewellery. Eight generations of the Mosun family have carried this torch, each adding their own chapter to a story written entirely in gold.

In 1983, JKJ began exporting rich traditional Rajasthani Jewelry to UAE and other countries, bringing the craftsmanship of Rajasthan to the world stage. The first Jaipur showroom opened in 1996 on the iconic M I Road, under the leadership of Satyanarayan Ji Mosun.

World Gold Council Recognition

First to Introduce 22/22 Hallmark Jewellery in India

JKJ Jewellers holds the distinction of being the first jeweller in India to introduce 22/22 Hallmark jewellery — a landmark in certified purity. This pioneering achievement was recognised and awarded by the World Gold Council, cementing JKJ's position at the forefront of quality and transparency in the Indian jewellery industry.
